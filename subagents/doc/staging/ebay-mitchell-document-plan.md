# eBay Mitchell Audit Deliverables Plan

## Objective
Create polished, user-facing deliverables for the eBay Mitchell audit.

## Audience
- Internal stakeholders
- Reviewers of the Mitchell audit findings
- Decision-makers needing a concise summary and supporting detail

## Planned Deliverables
1. Executive summary document
2. Detailed audit report
3. Optional slide deck summary for presentation

## Source Material Needed
- Audit notes and findings
- Any supporting spreadsheets or raw evidence
- Scope, timeline, and stakeholder expectations

## Proposed Structure

### 1. Executive Summary
- Purpose
- Key findings
- Risk / impact summary
- Recommended next steps

### 2. Detailed Audit Report
- Background
- Scope and methodology
- Findings by category
- Evidence / examples
- Risks and implications
- Recommendations
- Appendix

### 3. Slide Outline
- Title
- Audit objective and scope
- Key findings
- Highest-priority risks
- Recommendations
- Next steps

## Tone and Style
- Professional
- Clear and concise
- Executive-friendly with enough detail for review

## Destination
- Local rendered outputs in `outputs/`
- Working drafts in `staging/`

## Immediate Next Step
Convert this plan into a structured content spec after gathering the available source materials for the audit.
